--- title: "Shaping Low-Rank Recurrent Neural Networks with Biological Learning Rules" excerpt: "Biologically-plausible training of low-rank RNNs - with Pablo Crespo, Dimitra Maoutsa, Matt Getz
" collection: portfolio --- We study how different plasticity rules affect the low-dimensional representations and dynamics of low-rank recurrent neural networks, and employ simulation based inference within a teacher-student framework to identify plasticity rules that solve common neuroscience tasks. The low-dimensional representions of gradient-descent trained teacher networks serve as activity characterisers for infering biologically-plausible plasticity rules that imprint these low-dimentional representations onto the student networks.
